TradeBuza X Unitrust Insurances

One long-standing challenge that has hindered growth in the agricultural sector is the risk of capital loss.

When financiers can access credible information about farmers and their farmlands, they are more likely to invest in agricultural projects that will impact the lives of smallholder farmers and the entire agri-value chain.

In line with our mission to advance financial inclusion for farmers, we are thrilled to announce an exciting partnership with Unitrust Insurance Limited.

This partnership represents a unique opportunity for both companies to deliver tremendous benefits to farmers and the broader agriculture industry.

At TradeBuza, we are dedicated to leveraging technology to solve some of the biggest challenges facing the agriculture industry. Our solutions are designed to help farmers improve productivity, reduce costs, increase yields, and boost financial inclusion for smallholder farmers.

Unitrust Insurance Limited has been providing specialized insurance solutions to the agriculture industry for years. They offer insurance products that address the risks associated with farming and are designed specifically for farmers.

Together, we will be combining our strengths to enable farmers to make more informed decisions about everything from planting and harvesting to managing their risk exposure.

In addition, this partnership will also allow us to reach more farmers and expand our services to new regions. We are incredibly excited about the possibilities of this new partnership and look forward to working together to drive innovation and progress in the agriculture industry.
